FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Your Queries Answered

What is a virtual office?

A virtual office is your physical office space and business address which you may use for all your business communication without having a dedicated space. Virtual offices are increasingly becoming popular among start ups, freelancers, new business registrations, established business for extension of their branches.

What documents shall be Provided to me with Virtual Office?

Rent Agreement on Stamp Paper
NOC - No Objection Certificate for Business Registration
Utility Bill for Address Proof of the office

What documents require to get Virtual Office?

Pan Card of the Owner of the business/All Partners taking space at Ideashacks Coworking.
Government Issued IDs of all the members taking desks at Ideashacks for 2 days in a month. GST number if already have.
ROC certificate post-registration at Ideashacks Coworking Address.
All agreements Duly Signed and Stamped
Company Profile

Can a GST number be obtained on a Virtual Office address?

Yes, with proper documentation, GST number can be obtained with virtual office, Address proof, Agreement and NOC from legal owner is provided.

What are the benefits of a virtual office?

Cost effective business registration and GST registration with access to meeting room, conference room, event area and day pass for workstation in Coworking lounge.

Do I need a virtual office?

Perhaps your company has grown beyond the needs of a Virtual Office, and you need a physical office to house staff and equipment. A Virtual Office gives your company the professional presentation it needs, gives you the freedom to still explore and work anywhere you need to, all while saving you money.

Can you register a company at a virtual office?

Yes, a business need not trade from its registered office – as long as any documents served at the address are delivered to the company directors. However, if you would like to use our address as a registered office, you can pick up our registered office service.

How can IDEASHACKS help you get the best virtual offices as per your requirement across PAN India?

IDESHACKS is a prominent player in providing virtual offices across PAN India. IDEASHACKS have so many beautiful virtual offices in the major cities in India. You have plenty of options to choose from.

Are Virtual offices legal?

There is nothing illegal about using a virtual office. Many business either do not want a physical office or cannot afford one, but do not want to be seen as working from home. This has become especially true since the rise of COVID and with e-commerce companies seeking legitimacy.

Can a Virtual Office be used as a physical address?

Often yes, but it depends on the definition of a physical address. Virtual offices are themselves in physical buildings with an address shared by many, but you may not be physically present. Ultimately, it depends on who is demanding the physical address and what they mean by it.
